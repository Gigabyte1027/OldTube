Welcome!

This is a version of YouTube based heavily off of touhotaku1's Invidious Mobile project.
The main goal of this webapp is to get old iOS 5.x and Android devices to watch youtube.
There is no account system, and I have no plans to impliment one. All subscriptions and anything else i may add in the future are purely client side and will not sync across devices.
If you would like to run YouTube on an older iDevice (iPod Touch 1g for example) do check out Invidious Mobile. It was designed specifically for iOS 1.1.

Happy watching! -Gigabyte1027
